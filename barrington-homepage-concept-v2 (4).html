What's Included in Every Tuition

More than childcare.
A complete childhood.

Most childcare centers charge extra for the things that matter most. At Barrington, they're already part of every child's day.

Prepared daily

Chef-Prepared Organic Meals

An on-site chef prepares fresh, organic breakfast, lunch, and snacks every morning. No lunchbox needed — your child eats well, every day.

In tuition

Diapers, Wipes & Essentials

Diapers, wipes, bibs, and blankets are covered in monthly tuition. One less thing to pack — and no line-item surprises on your bill.

Every week

Five Weekly Enrichment Classes

Gymnastics, soccer, yoga, dance, and music are built into your child's schedule. No extra fees, no separate sign-ups.

Every outdoor activity

Sunscreen Application

Staff apply sunscreen before every outdoor session. The small details add up — we handle them so you don't have to track them.

Below Ohio minimum

Low Student-to-Teacher Ratios

Our ratios are lower than Ohio requires. Individual attention isn't a bonus here — it's the standard every classroom is built around.

Always organic

Whole, Organic Food Philosophy

Every ingredient is organic. Every meal made fresh. We believe what children eat shapes how they learn, grow, and feel each day.

Our Story

Fifty years of caring for Columbus families

Barrington School started as a single location built on a simple belief: children deserve more than supervision. They deserve to be known.

That belief guides every decision we make — from the food we serve to the size of our classrooms. We are family-owned across all 12 locations, which means every director has a personal stake in your child's experience.

Today we serve thousands of Columbus-area families, but we still measure success the same way: does your child run toward the door in the morning?
