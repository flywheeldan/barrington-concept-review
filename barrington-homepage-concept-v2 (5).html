Our Approach

Care this considered is rare. We think it should be the standard.

For over fifty years, Barrington has been family-owned and family-run — and it shows in the details most schools overlook. Fresh, organic food made on-site. Smaller classes than the state requires. A full week of enrichment built into every child's day.

The result is a place where children are genuinely known, gently challenged, and cared for the way you would care for them yourself.
